Common fabric guide

Cotton

Soft, breathable, perfect for everyday wear. Made from the fluffy part of a cotton plant.

Denim

Strong and durable, used for jeans. Made from weaved cotton.

Leather

Tough and stylish, needs special care. Made from skins of cattle :'(

Silk

Smooth, shiny, and formal. Made from fibroin.

Wool

Warm and cozy, great for colder months. Made from the fleece of sheep.

Linen

Lightweight and breathable, but durable. Made from the fibers of the flax plant.

Polyester

Affordable and wrinkle-resistant. Made from polyethylene terephthalate, which is a type of plastic.

Velvet

Soft and luxurious with a rich texture.Historically made form silk, but you can regularly fidn it made with polyester or rayon in modern shops.

Fleece

Warm, soft, and casual. Made with polyester.

Satin

Smooth and glossy, often used in dresses. Made using silk or polyester.

Rayon

Soft and smooth, made with wood pulp.

Nylon

Elastic and silky. Made from petrochemicals.
